Last updated: February 4, 2025

At AmiCodes, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name
  • Project Details: Website requirements, business information, content you provide
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), TikTok user ID (if using AI tools)

Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Analytics: Google Analytics data to improve our services
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy below

AI-Generated Content:

  • Content you generate using our AI tools
  • Prompts and inputs you provide
  • Usage patterns to improve AI models

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Deliver web development, AI tools, and consulting services
  • Communication: Send project updates, invoices, and service notifications
  • Improvement: Analyze usage to enhance our services and AI models
  • Authentication: Connect with TikTok and other platforms on your behalf
  • Support: Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Marketing: Send promotional emails (you can opt out anytime)
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory and legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Data With:

  • TikTok: Only when you explicitly authorize posting content
  • OpenAI: For AI content generation (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  • Cloudflare: For CDN, security, and DDoS protection
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (anonymized data)
  • Payment Processors: To process payments securely
  • Hosting Providers: To host and deliver our services

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your content for purposes other than providing services
  • Post to your social media without explicit permission

We May Disclose Information:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or property
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Secure Storage: Encrypted databases for sensitive information
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and updates
  • Token Protection: TikTok and social media access tokens stored securely
  • HTTPS: All pages served over secure connections

Note: While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Active Accounts: As long as your account is active
  • Project Files: For 2 years after project completion (for support purposes)
  • AI-Generated Content: Until you request deletion
  • Financial Records: As required by law (typically 7 years)
  • Marketing Data: Until you opt out or request deletion

After retention periods, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Data Protection)

Under GDPR and other data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Object: Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time
  • Disconnect: Revoke TikTok and social media access anytime

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@amicodes.com
